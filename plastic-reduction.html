🧴 How to Reduce Plastic Waste

Simple steps to reduce plastic use and protect the planet.

🌍 Why Plastic Reduction Matters

Plastic pollution harms wildlife, oceans, and ecosystems. Reducing plastic use helps protect the environment and reduces long-term damage.

✅ Practical Tips

  • Use reusable bags and bottles
  • Avoid single-use plastics
  • Buy in bulk to reduce packaging
  • Recycle properly
  • Choose eco-friendly alternatives
