🌍 Environmental Awareness

Understanding the problem is the first step toward meaningful change.

Key Topics to Understand

  • Climate change and global warming
  • Plastic pollution and ocean impact
  • Carbon footprint and emissions
  • Energy consumption and renewable energy
  • Water scarcity and conservation

Why Awareness Matters

When people understand environmental issues, they are more likely to take action. Awareness drives change at individual, community, and global levels.
